Top carreer option after 12th from Commerce

Chartered Accountancy (CA)

Chartered Accountancy (CA)

Chartered Accountancy (CA) is a professional course that is highly sought after by commerce students. This course is conducted by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) and is considered to be one of the toughest professional courses in the country. CA course involves rigorous training and education in areas like accounting, taxation, auditing, financial management, and corporate laws. After completing the course and passing the exams, students can work as independent auditors, tax consultants, financial advisors, or in the finance departments of top companies, banks, or government agencies. CA course offers excellent career prospects and a high salary package, making it a popular choice among commerce students.
Qualification:Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or Finance; Certified Accountant (CA, CMA, or CPA) preferred
Average Salary in India:INR 3-10 lakhs per year

Company Secretary (CS)

Company Secretary (CS)

Company Secretary (CS) is another popular professional course for commerce students. This course is conducted by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) and provides specialized knowledge and training in corporate laws, governance, and secretarial practices. CS course involves three levels of exams, including foundation, executive, and professional programs. After completing the course and passing the exams, students can work as company secretaries, corporate lawyers, legal advisors, or in the compliance departments of top companies. CS course offers a high salary package and excellent career growth opportunities, making it a lucrative option for commerce students.
Qualification:Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or finance
Average Salary in India:INR 3-6 lakhs per annum.

(FA)

Financial Analyst (FA)

If you have strong analytical skills and are interested in financial markets, a career as a financial analyst may be an excellent option for you. Financial analysts analyze financial data to provide insights into market trends and investment opportunities. Many financial analysts work for investment banks, insurance companies, and other financial institutions.
Qualification:Bachelor's Degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field; Master's Degree in Finance or Business Administration (MBA) preferred
Average Salary in India: INR 4-9 lakhs per year

Business Development Executive

Business Development Executive

If you have excellent communication skills and enjoy networking, a career as a business development executive may be an excellent option for you. Business development executives are responsible for building relationships with potential clients, promoting products or services, and identifying new business opportunities.
Qualification:Bachelor's Degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field; Master's Degree in Finance or Business Administration (MBA) preferred
Average Salary in India: INR 2-7 lakhs per year

Sales and Marketing Executive

Sales and Marketing Executive

If you have good communication skills and enjoy convincing people, a career in sales and marketing could be an excellent choice for you. Sales and marketing executives are responsible for promoting products and services, identifying new customer opportunities, and maintaining relationships with existing clients.
Qualification:Bachelor's Degree in Business, Marketing, or related field
Average Salary in India: 2-5 lakhs INR per year..

Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship

If you have an entrepreneurial mindset and are interested in starting your own business, a career as an entrepreneur could be an excellent option for you. With the right idea and execution, you can build a successful business that meets the needs of your target market. You may also consider pursuing an MBA in entrepreneurship to gain valuable skills and knowledge needed to start and manage a business successfully.
Qualification:No specific education requirements;
Average Salary in India:Depends on the success of the business

Human Resources (HR) Manager

Human Resources (HR) Manager

If you enjoy working with people and are interested in managing the workforce of a company, a career in HR management could be an excellent option for you. HR managers are responsible for hiring, training, and developing employees, managing employee relations, and ensuring compliance with labor laws and regulations.
Qualification:Bachelor's or Master's Degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field.
Average Salary in India:4-12 lakhs INR per year.

Investment Banker

Investment Banker

If you have strong analytical skills and an interest in finance, a career as an investment banker could be an excellent option for you. Investment bankers work with clients to raise capital, acquire companies, and provide strategic financial advice.
Qualification:Bachelor's or Master's Degree in Finance, Business Administration, or related field; Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) certification preferred
Average Salary in India:8-20 lakhs INR per year.
